Freediving

What is Freediving?

Photo by Jakob Boman on Unsplash

Freediving is a popular form of diving done by many people. Similar to snorkeling, it is based on holding your breath
and diving down into the ocean. Contrary to popular belief, snorkeling is not the same as freediving. Freedivers usually
hold their breath for longer, and are known quite well for it. Herbert Nitsch, an Austrian freediver that holds the title of freediving world champion, can hold his breath for nine minutes.

Gear Needed

  • Wetsuit
  • Weight Belt
  • Long Fins
  • Dive Gloves
  • Long Fin Socks
  • Mask and Snorkel

SCUBA

What is SCUBA?

Photo by eileen byrne on Unsplash

SCUBA(Self Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus) diving is pretty straightforward. It is a form of diving done
with an air tank (hence the name SCUBA) as well as tons of other gear. SCUBA, although fun, can be dangerous if done
incorrectly, so you must always stay alert and not fool around.

Gear Needed

  • Wetsuit
  • Weight Belt
  • Jet Fins
  • Dive Gloves
  • Dive Boots
  • Mask and Snorkel
  • BC Jacket
  • Regulator (primary regulator, octopus, or both)
